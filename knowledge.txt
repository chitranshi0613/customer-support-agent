TaskFlow SaaS - Customer Support Knowledge Base

PRICING & BILLING
- Free plan: up to 3 projects, 5 team members, 1GB storage
- Pro plan: $12/month per user, unlimited projects, 50GB storage
- Business plan: $25/month per user, SSO, priority support, 500GB storage
- Billing date is the same day each month as when you first subscribed
- To cancel: go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription
- Refunds are available within 7 days of any charge, no questions asked
- We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and bank transfer for Business plans

ACCOUNT & ACCESS
- To invite a team member: Settings > Team > Invite by Email
- Team member roles: Admin, Editor, Viewer
- To reset your password: click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- If you're locked out after 5 failed attempts, wait 30 minutes or contact support
- To delete your account: Settings > Account > Delete Account (irreversible)

FEATURES
- Tasks can be assigned to multiple team members
- Deadlines trigger email reminders 24 hours before due date
- You can export any project as CSV or PDF from the project menu
- Integrations available: Slack, Google Drive, Notion, GitHub
- Mobile app available on iOS and Android

COMMON ISSUES
- App loading slow: clear browser cache or try incognito mode
- Email notifications not arriving: check spam folder, whitelist noreply@taskflow.io
- File upload failing: check your storage limit under Settings > Storage
- Integration not connecting: re-authenticate from Settings > Integrations

ESCALATION TRIGGERS
- User mentions "lawyer", "refund refused", "data breach", "lost all data"
- User has contacted support more than 3 times about the same issue
- User is clearly very angry or using aggressive language
- Billing dispute over $100
